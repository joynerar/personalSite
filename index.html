Armstrong Student Involvement Tracker

This project my team created an android application that logs the amount of hours that students volunteer at armstrong student center by signing in and out of an android tablet fixed to the wall. Organization leaders were then able to log into a web portal that displayed different reports such as average student involvement, weekly hours logged, total hours and others.

BuyerBorard

This is a php laravel project MVP. The intent of the business plan was to operate like ebay or amazon, but in reverse. So, people looking for items were able to post a name, description and sample images of an item they were looking to buy, then sellers were able to log in to the website and search for items they own.
